How to Ride A Bike

Sit On Bicycle
First do a safety check
  1. Are you wearing a helmet
  2. Are you dressed appropriatly "Pro Kit"
  3. Are your socks the correct length
  4. Are you tires inflated
  5. Is your bike chain clean

becoming comfortable is your first step

Lift kickstand

This is a crucial step

Straighten handlebars

make sure to look where you are going your bicycle will follow your eyes

Place foot on pedal

centering the bike beneith you is an important step

Pedal with other foot

creating momentum and following through will get you where you need to go

Be Safe and Enjoy Cycling
